== Description ==

Welcome to InnovateGrove, the ultimate WordPress Full Site Editing theme designed for businesses, consulting firms, and SEO agencies looking to enhance their online presence. Optimized for superior SEO and fully compatible with both Gutenberg and WooCommerce, InnovateGrove makes it easy to create a dynamic, growth-focused website. Its block-based customization and seamless integration with Contact Form 7 offer unparalleled flexibility, enabling you to showcase your services, engage with clients, and expand your reach effortlessly.
